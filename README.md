# 📊 Global Electronics & Furniture Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze the 2024 sales performance of a multinational Electronics and Furniture company. 
This project demonstrates Excel's data analysis and visualization capabilities by transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was developed using **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales transactions across multiple regions, products, and salespersons.

The dashboard helps management monitor key business metrics, identify top-performing regions and products, evaluate salesperson performance, and understand monthly sales trends.

---




## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard answers the following business questions:

- Which region should receive more investment?
- Which products should be promoted or discontinued?
- Who are the top-performing salespeople?
- Are Electronics more profitable than Furniture?
- What seasonal sales trends exist?

## 💡 Business Insights

### 🌍 Regional Performance

- Asia generated the highest sales and profit.
- North America showed strong business performance.
- Europe contributed comparatively lower sales.
- Asia has the highest growth potential and should receive additional investment.

---

### 📦 Product Analysis

- Smartphones generated the highest revenue.
- Laptops ranked second in sales.
- Desks recorded the lowest sales.
- Smartphones and Laptops should be promoted further.
- Lower-performing products should be reviewed for improvement.

---

### 👨‍💼 Salesperson Performance

- Charlie achieved the highest overall sales performance.
- Alice and Bob delivered consistent sales performance.
- Charlie contributed significantly to overall revenue.

---

### 💻 Category Analysis

- Electronics generated higher revenue than Furniture.
- Electronics contributed more to overall profitability.

---

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend

- Sales peaked during July and August.
- February recorded the lowest sales.
- Mid-year demand was significantly stronger than the first half of the year.
